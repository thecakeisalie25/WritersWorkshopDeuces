Should every child be forced to serve in the millitary?

In combat, I would say no. A definite no. But during times of peace, or for non-war roles, I'd say it's not that bad of an idea. It could allow them to skip all the boring first-time jobs that we require here in america, like working at mcdonalds or burger king, and allow them to jump straight into work with the right skills to do so in a real environment.
